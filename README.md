# Your website is designed as a simple, clean, OPPO-style personal platform where you can upload your blogs, photos, and personalized notes. It acts as your own digital space on the internet, built using HTML and hosted for free on GitHub Pages.


---

1️⃣ Purpose of the Website

Your website serves as:

A personal blog platform

A photo gallery

A notes-sharing space

A place to build your online brand


It helps you showcase your creativity, ideas, and knowledge.


---

2️⃣ Design & Theme

You chose an OPPO-inspired theme:

Fresh green color

Clean and modern design

Minimal and professional layout

Simple card-based structure


The website feels like a tech brand page, giving you a premium look.


---

3️⃣ Website Structure

Your homepage contains 3 main sections:

✔️ Blog Section

You can post stories, experiences, tech updates, study ideas, or personal thoughts.

Each blog card has a title, description, and image.


✔️ Photo Gallery Section

For uploading your photos (tech, lifestyle, nature, gaming, etc.)

You only need to replace the image link to update photos.


✔️ Personal Notes Section

Adds study notes or personal reminders.

Good for Class 10 notes, formulas, chapter summaries, etc.



---

Your website is completely built with:

HTML

CSS

100% free
